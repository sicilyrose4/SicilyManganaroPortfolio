Case Study

Rebound Physical Therapy

Rebound is a physical therapy app that helps teenage athletes stay on top of their rehab, track their progress, and rebuild confidence as they recover from injury and return to the sport they love.

Problem Statement

How might we help high school athletes in rehab from a sports related injury stay motivated, track meaningful progress, and maintain consistent, engaging exercise routines?

Insights from Secondary Research

Physical Challenges
Pain, fatigue, stiffness, and limited mobility are common, but growth-plate and overuse injuries are the biggest concerns. Psychological and social barriers often impact rehab adherence more than physical ones.
Psychological Challenges
Athletes often bridge poor communication between coaches and providers. Injury can lead to exclusion, identity loss, and isolation. Repetitive rehab and negative comparisons decrease motivation.
Practical Barriers
School, therapy, and activities create tight time constraints. Limited access to specialized care makes rehab harder. Communication gaps between coaches, providers, and athletes add stress and uncertainty.
Support Factors
Emotional support and positive feedback boost motivation. Individualized, specific rehab plans keep athletes engaged. Peer support reduces isolation and anxiety. Mental-health resources, goal tracking, and progress visualization improve adherence.

Competitive Analysis

Features

  • Onboarding questionnaire personalizes experience
  • Phases shown as "achievements" — encourages completion
  • Different programs for specific injuries

Areas to Improve

Simplify onboarding with clearer steps and personalized injury inputs. Strengthen progress tracking with clearer visuals and motivational milestones. Boost emotional support through positive copy.

Features

  • Advanced recommendation algorithm
  • Progress tracking / streaks / analytics
  • Personal favorites / library / saved exercises
  • Convenience & habit building

Areas to Improve

Assessment & recommendation may still be generic vs individual in-person evaluation. Wish Discover had a community tab. Reliance on user self-motivation and correct execution.
